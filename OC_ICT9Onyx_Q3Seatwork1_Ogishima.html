Kamome Academy is famous for its rumors about the seven wonders and supernatural events. Summoning her, Nene discovered that "Hanako-san" had nothing to do with the rumors; Hanako-san is a boy. Along the way, Nene discovers her connection to the spirit world and the dark secrets surrounding Hanako and her past.
